# Tic Tac Toe

A simple 3x3 Tic Tac Toe game for two players on one device.

## Users
Casual gamers who want to play a quick game together on the same screen.

## Brand & Tone
Fun, clean, minimal. Friendly and approachable.

## Core Features
- 3x3 game board
- Two players alternate turns (X and O)
- Automatic win/draw detection
- Reset/new game button
- Show whose turn it is
- Highlight winning combination
