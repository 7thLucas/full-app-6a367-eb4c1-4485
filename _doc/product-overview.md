# Tic Tac Toe

## Product
Simple browser-based 6×6 Tic Tac Toe game for two players on one device. No downloads, no accounts, no setup — open and play.

## Users
Casual gamers — two people sharing one screen who want a quick, frictionless game together.

## Problem
No instant option for two people to play a classic game on one shared screen. Most digital versions need downloads, accounts, or only offer single-player AI.

## Solution
Tic Tac Toe delivers a zero-friction, browser-based 2-player game. Open URL → play immediately.

## Core Features
- **6×6 interactive board** — tap any cell to place a mark; get 4 in a row to win
- **Two-player alternating turns** — X goes first, then O
- **Automatic win detection** — rows, columns, diagonals; winning cells highlighted in amber
- **Draw detection** — auto-detected when all cells filled with no winner
- **Per-session score tracker** — X Wins / Draws / O Wins persisted during the session
- **Reset button** — new round without clearing session scores

## Brand & Tone
Fun, clean, minimal. Friendly and approachable.
- Player X color: Blue `#2563eb`
- Player O color: Red `#ef4444`
- Win highlight: Amber
- Heading font: Poppins
- Body font: Inter

## Configurable Fields
`gameTitle`, `gameSubtitle`, `playerXLabel`, `playerOLabel`, `playerXColor`, `playerOColor`, `winHighlightColor`, `resetButtonLabel`

## Strategic Principles
- Zero friction: no accounts, no downloads, instant play
- Human vs human first: two players on one device
- Session score persistence drives competitive replay
